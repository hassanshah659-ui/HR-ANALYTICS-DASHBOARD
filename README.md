# HR Employee Attrition Analytics Dashboard

## Project Overview

This project presents an interactive HR Employee Attrition Analytics Dashboard developed using Tableau. The dashboard transforms raw employee data into meaningful visual insights, enabling HR managers and business leaders to monitor workforce trends, understand attrition drivers, and identify high-risk employee segments.

The solution focuses on analyzing employee demographics, job roles, satisfaction levels, compensation, tenure, and risk indicators to support informed workforce management decisions.

## Business Problem

Employee attrition is a major challenge for organizations as it leads to increased recruitment costs, productivity loss, and knowledge gaps within teams. Human Resource departments require data-driven insights to identify the factors influencing employee turnover and develop effective retention strategies. However, large HR datasets often make it difficult to quickly detect patterns related to employee satisfaction, compensation, tenure, and workplace conditions.

## Solution

A comprehensive Tableau dashboard was designed to provide a centralized view of workforce analytics and attrition trends. Through interactive visualizations and dynamic filters, users can explore employee behavior across different departments, job roles, education fields, and business travel categories.

The dashboard helps decision-makers:

* Identify high-risk employee groups.
* Monitor attrition trends across the organization.
* Evaluate the impact of satisfaction, income, overtime, and tenure on employee turnover.
* Support strategic HR planning and retention initiatives.

## Key Insights

* Employees with lower job satisfaction levels exhibit higher attrition rates.
* Overtime employees are significantly more likely to leave the organization.
* Attrition risk decreases as employee tenure increases.
* Certain departments and job roles experience higher turnover compared to others.
* Employees with lower monthly income show greater attrition tendencies.
* Workforce risk segmentation enables identification of employees requiring retention attention.
